About Story

Mr. Harsh Datta, the founder and owner of the organization named Story, established in the year 2012. He has been always relentless about the way market scenario is been especially in the service sector. He started exploring the market and the ways it catered to its customers. Hence, he was not satisfied with the traditional ways of functioning, so after observing the lack of quality and design in it, he traversed new ways to get a better understanding of an international flourished market. After an in-depth study, the company approached the Chinese & Thai market and started trading with different vendors and realized that design and quality can be implemented in the Indian market.

Henceforth, to deliver the international standard to the Indian market and to introduce it to the Indian customer, Story was adamant to work towards it. After giving 6 years of learning and adapting, Story was successful in providing the international standards in the best of price with excellence. It’s first child company named Lovisha, was introduced in the year 2013 and was running with tremendous growth, providing quality in clothing, accessory and footwear.

Story was all about experimenting and taking risk, so Harsh took a plunge and diversified into a whole new market which was food and beverage industry. After observing the international trends of the market and utilization of the space, Story wanted to adapt those rituals and techniques to the Indian market, thus an exuberant opening of Café Vince in year 2018, brought unexpected success, this gave a brief idea about the projection of the people residing in Ahmedabad, within 1.5 years it was a successfully running venture.

In the year 2019, Muse bistro was launched as another child company, that catered service with a new dash of taste and introduced fusion food. People cherished the taste and ambience of the place. It was a victory within 2 months of period, as Muse was nominated in the category of Best Coffee Lounge and Best Café of City by The Times Food Awards.Story endorses in two food chains and two cloth retail stores with exponential growth rate.
